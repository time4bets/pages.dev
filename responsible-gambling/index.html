Responsible Play

Last revised: May 2026

Time4Bets content is intended for adults who treat betting and casino games as entertainment. This page was rewritten to place practical control tools and warning signs in a clearer order.

1. Keep Play Recreational

Set a budget before depositing, decide how long you plan to play and avoid using money needed for bills, rent, food or family responsibilities.

2. Warning Signs

Risk signs include chasing losses, hiding gambling, borrowing money to play, ignoring limits, gambling while stressed or feeling unable to stop.

3. Limit Tools

Players may use deposit limits, session reminders, loss limits or cool-off periods where available. Limits work best when set before play becomes emotional.

4. Self-Exclusion

Self-exclusion can block account access for a chosen period. It should be considered when gambling no longer feels controlled or recreational.

5. Adult Protection

Accounts should not be shared with minors. Devices should be locked and payment details should not be saved on shared browsers.

6. Support

If gambling causes stress, debt, relationship problems or loss of control, stop playing and contact professional support services in your region.
